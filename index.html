Welcome To IKhwezi Lomso Funeral Home

We are leaders in the funeral undertaking business in Uitenhage, Port Elizabeth and surrounding areas


IKhwezi Lomso Funeral Home is a fledging funeral undertaking business providing excellent service to individual and family members from all walks of life. It has built business relationship with stake holders in the entire province of the Eastern Cape while maintaining industry standards on operational excellence and product development. The business has established itself through following current regulations and meets all the compliance requirements in terms of current legislation.

Our Services

Coffins

Our coffins are expertly designed to provide a serene resting place, reflecting the individuality and unique character of those we lay to rest.

Caskets

Our caskets are thoughtfully designed to embody reverence and respect, honoring the unique life and legacy of each individual.

Tombstones

Each tombstone can be customized to reflect the individuality and personal preferences of the departed, allowing family and friends to create a meaningful and enduring tribute.

Coffin Spray

Our talented florists create elegant and custom-designed coffin sprays that serve as a stunning tribute to the life and legacy of the departed.

Grocery Package For Every Burial Policy

Upon completion of the burial service, we provide a complimentary grocery package to help alleviate some of the immediate burdens families may face.

Grave Site Decoration

With meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to creating a tranquil atmosphere, we strive to provide solace and a peaceful space for remembrance, allowing family and friends to pay their respects in a meaningful way.
